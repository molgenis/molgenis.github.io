About

Information about MOLGENIS, publications, team and history.

    MOLGENIS is an open-source software under LGPLv3 License which is currently coordinated by the Genomics Coordination Center hosted at the Dept of Genetics of University Medical Center Groningen. It exists since 2002 but has been completely rewritten several times to keep up with the rapid changes in the scientific world.
